My Story

I’ve always been a mechanically inclined, have a need to know how things work and I’m not afraid to dive head first into complicated tasks. I first discovered my love of code when building a website for a real estate school where I worked. We needed a new website, but we didn’t have the budget to pay for what I envisioned. My solution was to do it myself. I knew that I could acquire the skills I needed with the help of the Internet and the knowledge that I don’t give up easily. Within 2 weeks I built the website, made it mobile responsive, SEO optimized and capable of handling ecommerce transactions. This gave me the confidence to pursue more complicated tasks. Next, I built a relational database with Filemaker to maintain student records and enrollments and later learned Swift to program a simple math calculator that I planned to use as a marketing piece. This simple calculator which you'll see above evolved into a full scale application that calculates not just math, but also date calculations, mortgage calculations and prorates. Building this application was an amazing opportunity to dig deep into modern programming techniques like working in an IDE, using external libraries, source control, developing an efficient UX/UI, testing and debugging code.

My professional experience over that past 14 years has been in the real estate industry, where I am currently a REALTOR® and Associate Broker for Coldwell Banker. While it may seem that computer programming and real estate have little in common, I believe many skills translate easily. Preparing contracts must be done with precision and free of errors to withstand legal challenge. Marketing homes today rarely includes traditions of the past and favors new, highly technical means including social media, predictive analytics, listing syndication and ad retargeting. Even the sales cycle from contract negotiation to escrow closing requires careful communication between multiple channels with competing priorities. A strong willed dedication and ability to quickly solve problems is required to overcome the numerous conflicts that arise on a daily basis.
